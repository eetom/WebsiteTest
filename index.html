About Truth

Truth FAQ

Got questions? We got the answers

Why do I have to send a text to verify my account?

In order to verify your Truth! account we get you to send us a unique text from your mobile device to connect the account to the mobile number. Afterwards you will be able to read all the messages sent to that account.

I received some messages that I found were offensive. Is there a way to report this?

Yes, on the top right of the conversation screen is the report button. We do not condone offensive content in our app. See our Terms of Service to learn more.

When is it coming to (Android/WP/etc..) platform?

We're currently working around the clock to get the best experience for Truth on other platforms. We'll have more to update once we reach a stage we feel is right for release!

Are you going to add Picture messaging?

Another frequently requested feature! We're working on that as soon as we get the app on to more platforms! Stay tuned for more! Terms of Service to learn more.
